Momos are a popular street food in northern parts of India. These are also known as Dim Sum and are basically dumplings made from flour with a savory stuffing.
Learn to make these popular Tibetan recipe of easy veg momos from scratch. The momos recipe is also vegan.


Ingredients

For dough

  1. 1 cup all-purpose flour
  2. ½ teaspoon oil
  3. ¼ teaspoon salt or as required
  4. 2 to 3 tablespoon water for kneading or as required

For Vegetable Stuffing

  1. 1 tablespoon oil
  2. 2 small-sized spring onions (scallions) finely chopped
  3. ½ teaspoon finely chopped garlic or 3 to 4 small-sized garlic, finely chopped
  4. 1.5 to 1.75 cups finely chopped mix vegetables
  5. 1 teaspoon soy sauce or add as required
  6. ½ teaspoon black pepper powder or as required
  7. salt as required

For dough

Take flour and add salt.Then add hot oil and just work the dough.Then add water as required and knead for about 5 to 6 minutes
and let the dough rest for about 20 minutes

For stuffing

In a pan put 1 to 2 spoon oil and heat it.Then add garlic and saute it till it turns slightly brown.Then add onions and saute it
for a while till onion is half cooked.Then add all your vegetables,soy sauce and black pepper.Add salt only if you want
since soy sauce is already present.Then let the stuffing be cooled for a while.

Now take your dough and start making small balls out of them.Roll them into a cirle of about 3 to 4 cm radius and put your stuffing
in there.Then seal your momo however you want by rubbing water to edges of your cirle.
Then put your momos directly in boiling water or in a steaming pot. It takes about 15 to 20 min for cooking since our veggies are already cooked.
