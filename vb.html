Vegetable Biryani is a bold and flavorful Indian rice dish with bell peppers, peas, carrots and potatoes in a spiced
rice dish made with turmeric, garam masala and other warm spices.


Ingredients

For masala

  1. 3 tablespoons olive oil or ghee if available
  2. 2 to 3 bay leaf
  3. 2 to 3 cloves and cardamom
  4. 2 star anise
  5. 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  6. 1 spoon fennel seeds

For biryani

  1. 1 onion cut into 1/2-inch dice
  2. 1 tablespoon garlic minced
  3. 1 tablespoon ginger minced
  4. 1 roma tomato minced finely
  5. 1/2 cup water
  6. 1/2 cup peas
  7. 1 carrot sliced into thin coins
  8. 2 potatoes peeled and chopped
  9. 2 teaspoons salt
  10. 2 teaspoons garam masala
  11. 4 tbsp Biryani masala
  12. coriander leaves
  13. Mint leaves
  14. 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
  15. 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  16. 2 cups basmati rice rinsed and drained
  17. 1 glass milk
  18. 1 cup curd

Recipe

First fry the masala in the oil and ghee in a cooker. Then add ginger,garlic and onion and saute it till it turns golden
brown.Now add the veggies and stir till turns cruncy. Add curd to it and mix. Now add salt,turmeric,chilli powder,3 spoon biryani masala
and garam masala and saute. Add rice at this point and add water accordingly. Put chopped mint and coriander in to the mixture
and wait till it boils. Now add milk mixed with turmeric,1 spoon biryani masala,some mint and coriander and a spoon of ghee. Close
the lid and wait till it whistles once.
