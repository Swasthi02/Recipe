Yet another popular street food from the indo chinese recipe palette made from crisp and deep fried potato wedges or potato fries. it is crispy, sweet and spicy coated with honey, chilli sauce, vinegar and soy sauce which makes it a ideal snack or as a starters.


Ingredients

  1. 2 to 3 potatoes
  2. oil for frying
  3. 3 to 4 tablespoon Rice flour
  4. 2 small-sized onions chopped
  5. 3 to 4 small-sized garlic, finely chopped
  6. Chilly powder as required
  7. 1 teaspoon tomato ketchup or add as required
  8. 1 to 2 tomatoes chopped
  9. 2 tablespoon honey
  10. salt as required

For frying potatoes

Peel the potatoes and chop them like french fries and wash them in salt water to remove starch.
Then take riceflour and add a bit of salt and add potatoes to it. Toss the potatoes in the rice flour so that it gets evenly covered in rice flour.
Now in hot oil fry your potatoes till they turn golden brown. Put them in a plate to cool down.

For sauce

Take about 2 spoons of the tomato ketchup and add finely chopped garlic, chilly powder. Mix them into a watery paste consistency.

In a pan put 2 spoon oil and heat it.Then add onions and saute it till its half cooked. Then add tomatoes, our garlic
sauce and a bit of salt saute it till tomatoes are half cooked(we want that crunch). Then turn off the stove.
Now add fried potatoes and honey and mix it till the sauce is fully covered. Add coriander leaver for garnishing if you want.
